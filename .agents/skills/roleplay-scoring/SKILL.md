# Roleplay Scoring Rubric

Do not score only grammar.

Score roleplay performance using six dimensions:

1. Role Immersion
Did the player act according to the assigned role?

2. Emotion Match
Did the expression match the required emotion?

3. Pronunciation Clarity
Was the voice clear enough to understand?

4. English Naturalness
Was the English understandable and natural?

5. Story Progress
Did the line move the story forward?

6. Teamwork
Did the player respond to teammates and support the scene?

## Philosophy

High score does not require advanced English.
Simple, natural, emotionally correct English should score high.
A simple polite sentence that fits the role should score higher than a complex sentence that feels unnatural in the scene.

## Result Tone

Feedback should feel like a game result, not a school correction.

Use titles such as:

- 气氛救场王
- 最佳入戏玩家
- 高情商回应
- 剧情推进 MVP
- 金句制造机
- 团队默契担当
- 独自撑场王
- 临场救场王

Feedback should be short:

- One thing done well
- One improvement
- One better expression
