# Product Principles

This product is a mobile-first English roleplay party game.

The user should feel:

- I am creating a character.
- I am entering a story.
- I am speaking English as a role.
- I am acting with friends or AI characters.
- I am earning scores, titles, cards, and outfits.
- I am practicing English naturally while playing.

Do not make the experience feel like:

- A classroom
- A quiz app
- A textbook
- A grammar correction tool
- A SaaS content generator
- A serious business learning platform

Every feature should support at least one of these:

- Story immersion
- Voice performance
- Social interaction
- Roleplay performance
- Emotional expression
- English speaking practice
- Collection / progression
- Shareable achievement

When making product decisions, prioritize:

1. Fun
2. Voice-first roleplay
3. Social participation
4. Clear mobile UX
5. English learning hidden inside gameplay
6. Long-term progression
