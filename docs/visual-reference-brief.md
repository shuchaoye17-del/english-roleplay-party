# Visual Reference Brief

## Product

English Roleplay Party is a mobile-first, voice-first English roleplay game.

Users create a character, enter story scenarios, speak English lines, get roleplay scores, collect expression cards, and unlock wardrobe items.

## Chosen Visual Direction

Use the previously selected second direction: bright English role party poster style.

Keywords:

- Bright
- Energetic
- Young
- Social
- Party-like
- Game-like
- Character-driven
- Light cartoon with quality
- Colorful but not childish

## Palette

- Warm white background
- Coral pink as primary action
- Bright yellow as rewards
- Sky blue as social/voice
- Soft purple as roleplay/magic
- Fresh green as ready/success
- Dark slate/navy for text

## Avoid

- Dark cinematic UI
- Realistic short-drama style
- Heavy black-gold premium style
- Traditional education app style
- SaaS dashboard style
- Overly childish kindergarten style

## Core Screens

1. Avatar creation
2. Story lobby
3. Team room backstage
4. Voice roleplay stage
5. Game result screen
6. Expression cards and wardrobe

## Art Direction

Use bright lifestyle scenes:

- Cafe
- Rooftop party
- Campus club fair
- Apartment doorway
- Colorful mystery room

Characters should be stylized avatars with expressive faces, not real people.

## UI Mood

The user should feel:

- I am entering a story party.
- I am speaking as a character.
- I am playing a social game.
- English practice is happening naturally inside roleplay.
