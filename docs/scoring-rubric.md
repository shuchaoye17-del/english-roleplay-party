# Scoring Rubric

The app must not score only grammar.

Each result should feel like a game performance review, not a school exam.

## Dimensions

### 1. Role Immersion

Did the player act like the assigned character?

### 2. Emotion Match

Did the tone and expression match the required emotion?

### 3. Pronunciation Clarity

Was the spoken line clear enough to understand?

### 4. English Naturalness

Was the expression understandable and natural for the scene?

### 5. Story Progress

Did the line help move the story forward?

### 6. Teamwork

Did the player respond to other players and support the scene?

## Scoring Rules

- Simple natural English can score high.
- Advanced but unnatural English should not automatically score high.
- Rescue line turns should score at most 80.
- AI stand-in turns should score at most 50 for the absent player.
- Remaining players should not lose rewards because another player leaves.

## Result Titles

Examples:

- 气氛救场王
- 最佳入戏玩家
- 高情商回应
- 剧情推进 MVP
- 金句制造机
- 团队默契担当
- 独自撑场王
- 临场救场王

## Feedback Format

Each feedback block should be short:

- What went well
- What could be improved
- A better expression or reusable expression card
